What is The Mixer?

"We shape the future generation of history makers. The young professionals and entrepreneurs in Africa."

- Nicole Duma

Young Moguls Mixer is an initiative built to foster the formation of a community of young professionals and entrepreneurs from various backgrounds who come together to learn, form friendships/partnerships, and benefit from one another's experiences.

Our "mixers" create an environment that stimulates growth, builds confidence, and accelerates the success of young professionals and entrepreneurs through various professional networking events. We focus on self actualisation and development. Each young professional involved must leave armed with confidence that is tempered by the advice and guidance of their peers and select mentors, allowing them to navigate the corporate networking world with seniors in the game.



Our Values

Self Actualization

We promote the fufilment of one’s potential, the development of skills, and a greater appreciation for life.

Collaboration

We believe in working effectively with others, valuing diverse perspectives, and fostering teamwork to achieve common objectives.

Inspiration

We aim to motivate and energize the youth through positive interactions and encouraging them to strive for excellence.


"It is not the mountain we conquer but ourselves."

- Sir Edmund Hillary



Empowering young professionals through community, collaboration, and a shift in mindset towards achieving personal and professional success.

Our Vision

"The best way to predict the future is to create it."

- Peter Drucker

To shape a future where the youth confidently lead and innovate in business, transcending age barriers with resilience and determination as they make significant contributions to the corporate landscape.

Through a shift in perspective from "I’m too young to pull this off" towards one of strong self-worth and optimism, We believe that age is just a number and that the youth can establish a name and a place for themselves as entrepreneurs, business owners, and working professionals.



Our Mission

Self Actualization

To shape a future where young individuals confidently lead and drive innovation in the business world.

Breaking Age Barriers

To transcend age-related limitations through self determination.

Resilience and Determination

To build resilience to overcome challenges and inspiring a determined spirit to achieve goals.

Impactful Contribution

To cultivate a generation of history-makers as well as preparing young professionals for significant roles in business and society.


"Absorb what is useful, Discard what is not. Add what is uniquely you."

- Bruce Lee



To empower and unite young professionals and entrepreneurs through a supportive community where they can learn, collaborate, and thrive in order to succeed in the corporate world.
