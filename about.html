About Y.M.M.

A Dynamic Networking Hub for Young People

Business networking plays a crucial role in the growth and success of young entrepreneurs. It provides them with valuable opportunities to connect, collaborate, and learn from experienced professionals in their industry.

Young Moguls Mixer was founded as a response to the many times that young people feel intimidated during networking events when interacting with older, more established businessmen and women who have already made it in the corporate world.

The Big Why

"I hope that after attending these events, you feel inspired and connected to a supportive community"

- Nicole Duma

The motivation and idea behind the Young Moguls Mixer came to me after I had an interesting conversation with a young professional I met at a networking event. One of the things we agreed on, and the reason why we spent most of the event together was because we felt inexperienced. We found it quite difficult to engage in the numerous discussions held by people with considerably longer experience in the corporate sector. It wasn’t that they were unapproachable; rather, it was only that we felt...small.

It is because of this that young people often miss out on the genuine experience that networking offers. They feel inexperienced when engaging and interacting with other businessmen and women who have made it in the corporate world. My hope is that the Young Moguls Mixer will give young professionals and entrepreneurs the opportunity to interact with like-minded individuals who are aiming to become the next big names in the corporate world and are essentially on the same level as them.
